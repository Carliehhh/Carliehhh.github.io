Tom and Jerry Chasing Game

Game Rules

  • Use W, A, S, D keys to move Tom (the cat).
  • Catch Jerry (the mouse) to earn 5 points.
  • Eat the cheese to earn 10 points.
  • Avoid hitting obstacles, or you'll lose 5 points.
  • The game lasts for 60 seconds. Try to get the highest score!
